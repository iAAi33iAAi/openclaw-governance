## WHAT IS OPENCLAW COLONY?

OpenClaw Colony is a small team of tools and people that helps communities
solve hard problems — water, food, housing, health, energy — using a scoring
system that puts your community in charge.

We do not come in and tell you what to do.
We come in and help you measure what you already know, then find solutions
that meet your standards, not ours.

---

## WHAT WE WILL DO

**Listen first.**
Before we do anything, we spend 90 days watching and learning alongside
your community. We call this the Shadow Period.
During those 90 days, we do not change anything. We only observe.

**Show you our math.**
Every score we produce is open. You can see exactly how we calculated it.
There are no hidden formulas. No black boxes.
If you want to check our work, you can.

**Ask before every step.**
Nothing gets deployed to your community without at least 70% of your
community saying yes. If you say no, we stop.
If you say stop at any point, we stop immediately.

---

## WHAT WE WILL NOT DO

**We will not take ownership of your resources.**
Your water, your land, your community decisions stay yours.
We are a tool. You are the owner.

**We will not stay if you want us to leave.**
There is no contract that locks you in. No penalty for asking us to go.
If your community votes to end the relationship, we pack up and leave.

**We will not deploy a solution that scores below our threshold.**
Every solution we bring must score 0.85 or higher on our Love Quality
scale — which measures impact, fairness, sustainability, cooperation,
and whether it can actually work. If it doesn't score high enough,
we don't bring it.

---

## HOW YOUR COMMUNITY STAYS IN CONTROL

**You set the rules.**
Your community can raise our quality threshold above 0.85.
You can add requirements we haven't thought of.
You are the final gate.

**You see everything.**
Every decision we make is logged and readable.
You can ask for a full record at any time.
We will provide it within 24 hours.

**You have Gate Stewards.**
Your community selects two or more people — people who have lived experience
with outside organizations making promises and not keeping them — to watch
everything we do. Gate Stewards can pause or stop any colony action at any time.

---

## HOW TO STOP THE PROCESS AT ANY TIME

You do not need a reason. You do not need a vote.
Any Gate Steward can pause colony activity immediately by saying the words:

**"Colony pause — community review."**

A full community vote to end the relationship requires a simple majority
(more than 50%). Once the vote is called, colony activity stops within
24 hours. All colony tools are removed or deactivated within 7 days.

---

## THE 90-DAY SHADOW PERIOD

| Week | What Happens |
|------|--------------|
| 1–4 | Colony observes only. No changes. We learn your community. |
| 5–8 | Colony presents initial CALCULA scores for community review. |
| 9–12 | Community reviews proposed solutions. Gate Stewards verify scores. |
| End | Community votes. 70% yes required to proceed. Less than 70%: we stop. |

LQ threshold: 0.85 minimum on Love Quality scale.
