## MISSION

OpenClaw Colony is a sovereign AI operating system built to solve one problem:

**Forgotten people cannot find help. Help cannot find them.**

The global safety net is broken — not because resources don't exist, but because
the systems that should connect people to resources are fragmented, gatekept,
data-extractive, and inaccessible to those without smartphones, accounts, credit
cards, or technical literacy.

OpenClaw Colony is the answer. A fleet of AI agents, governed by the community,
powered by its own energy, carrying a covenant to its architect — that finds
people where they are and connects them to what they need. Free. Private. Sovereign.
